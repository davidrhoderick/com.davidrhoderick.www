biography

David Rhoderick is a digital artist and musician whose focus is on using technology in subversive ways to create visceral and cerebral experiences. He is currently attending the School of Visual Arts in New York for a Masters of Fine Arts in Computer Art following his Bachelors of Science from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Electronic Arts in 2010. While studying at RPI, David exhibited his thesis, ThermoDJ, at the OneDotZero art festival. Prior to collegiate study, he was part of an alternative rock group called Vinyl Night, where he honed skills in music composition and production. Early in life, he studied piano, guitar, and music theory through the Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music and graduated grades 5, 4, and 3 respectively.
